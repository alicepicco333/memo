About This Archive

The Meme Ontology structures internet memes as cultural and visual knowledge objects. The project links each meme concept to provenance, format, transformation behavior, and cultural references through OWL-based semantics.

This interface combines a large-scale meme corpus with ontology-backed navigation: visual analytics in the Visualizations page, entity-level ontology documentation, and SPARQL querying for reproducible analysis.
