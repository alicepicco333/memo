Datasets

Three interrelated datasets used in the Meme Ontology project.

5,000 Global Dataset Full meme collection · KYM
50 D0 Sample Richly annotated subset
391 Variant Dataset 50 with full annotations

Global Dataset

5,000 memes sourced from Know Your Meme, enriched with CLIP-based image classification and structured using the Meme Ontology OWL ontology.

5,000 memes

D0 — Annotated Sample

50 memes sampled from the global dataset with enriched metadata: descriptions, view counts, tags, and full ontology property annotations including image type, format, platform, region and time period.

50 memes

Variant Dataset

Meme variants scraped from Know Your Meme. A subset of 50 variants is annotated with transformation dimensions, extents, captions, and cultural references.
